Projects

Palmera

A mobile app designed to ease planning a trip with friends to the Caribbean.

We discovered that travelers search through a multitude of apps and websites to make plans for their vacations. In addition, they were often bogged down by the inability to plan and share all within one solution that it makes for an unpleasant experience.

Food Fighters

A mobile app intended to address hunger by reducing food insecurity while helping restaurants improve sustainability.

Create an app that helps to connect restaurants and grocery stores with organizations and volunteers that support people who are food insecure. We will be researching the pain points for donors, volunteers, and organizations in the process of donating surplus food to generate user insights. We will use these insights as a guide to navigate us through our ideation process to create and prototype our app.
